Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my own recipe?

We love receiving recipes from our community! Use the contact form above and select "Submit a Recipe" as the subject. Please include the dish name, ingredients, instructions, and any cultural background information.

Can I use your recipes for my restaurant or food business?

Our recipes are for personal use. For commercial inquiries, please contact us using the form above with "Partnership Inquiry" as the subject.

I can't find an ingredient listed in a recipe. What should I do?

Many African ingredients have substitutes! Contact us with the specific ingredient, and we'll suggest alternatives. We also provide substitution guides in many of our recipes.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

We're exploring virtual cooking classes! Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we launch this program.
